About Gynecology

Regular Services in obstetrics gynaecology outpatient, for pregnant, postnatal women and women with various gynaecological disorders including Infertility ,gynaecological cancers , Family planning services , Cervical cancer screening etc. for all age groups –

As per timings outpatient services, regular services in Obstetrics Gynaecology inpatient 24hours /7days for obstetric emergencies, high risk pregnancies, birthing women, postnatal women and women needing inpatient services for various gynaecological disorders, emergencies ,regular with open as well as mini invasive endoscopic surgeries, medication .

In addition speciality clinics, subspecialty clinics, to provide focussed care, from experts, and proper records will also be run –like

  1. Gynecologic Oncology
  2. Urogynaecology
  3. Reproductive Endocrinology and Infertility
  4. Maternal-Fetal Medicine
  5. Pediatric and Adolescent Gynecology
  6. Menopausal and Geriatric Gynecology

In addition Community based reproductive health services with concept of family care to rural population of Defined area by nurse midwives trained and monitored by departmental team

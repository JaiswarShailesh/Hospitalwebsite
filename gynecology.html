Obstetrics & Gynaecology

Comprehensive care for women's reproductive health, pregnancy, and childbirth through all stages of life.

  • Overview
  • Technology
  • Procedure

Overview

Gynecology focuses on the health and wellbeing of the female reproductive system across all stages of life.

It encompasses a wide range of services from routine examinations and screenings to managing complex reproductive disorders, prenatal care, and menopause-related concerns. Our gynecology department is dedicated to providing compassionate, confidential, and evidence-based care tailored to each woman’s unique needs.

Technology

We utilize advanced technologies to ensure accurate diagnosis and safe, minimally invasive treatments.

This includes ultrasound imaging for fetal monitoring and diagnosis, colposcopy for cervical assessment, hysteroscopy for uterine evaluation, and laparoscopic equipment for surgical interventions. Integration with electronic medical records ensures seamless follow-up and coordinated care. Our continuous investment in technology helps detect and manage conditions early—enhancing treatment outcomes and patient comfort.

Procedure

Every procedure is performed with utmost sensitivity, patient dignity, and clinical excellence.

Outpatient procedures include pap smears, contraceptive insertions, and screenings for infections or abnormalities. For surgical cases, minimally invasive techniques like laparoscopy and hysteroscopy are preferred to reduce recovery time and risk. Pre-procedure counseling is provided to address any concerns, and post-procedure follow-up ensures complete support. Our gynecologists collaborate closely with anesthesiologists and nursing teams to deliver safe care across both minor and major interventions.

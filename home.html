About Us

A modern healthcare institution committed to compassionate care, clinical excellence, and community well-being.

SVKM’s Tapanbhai Mukeshbhai Patel Memorial Hospital is a state-of-the-art healthcare facility dedicated to delivering compassionate, high-quality medical care. Located in Shirpur, Dhule, the hospital is equipped with modern infrastructure, advanced technology, and a team of skilled professionals committed to patient well-being. Guided by strong values and a vision for excellence, we aim to serve the community with integrity, innovation, and empathy

About SVKM's TM Patel Hospital

“Our healthcare journey commenced in 1951 by the late Shri P. D. Hinduja, with a vision to provide quality healthcare for all. Over the last seven decades, we have passionately followed this philosophy and today the hospital is regarded as one of the top hospitals in Mumbai and one of the best hospitals in India. We commit to move forward with greater zeal and determination to set new benchmarks in medical excellence and patient care.”

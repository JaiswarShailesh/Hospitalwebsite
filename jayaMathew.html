Jaya Mathew

OPD Incharge

Ms. Jaya Mathew is a dedicated and accomplished nursing professional with over 21 years of extensive experience in critical care and outpatient services. She joined SVKM’s Tapanbhai Mukeshbhai Patel Memorial Hospital as OPD Incharge on 10th April 2024, and continues to provide compassionate and efficient healthcare delivery in a structured and safety-driven environment.

Ms. Mathew holds strong leadership, patient communication, and operational management skills make her a valuable contributor to hospital excellence. Her career spans key roles in ICU, CCU, MICU, NICU, and ward management, with a proven track record in hospitals in Jammu & Jalandhar

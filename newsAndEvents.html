August’24: Mega Health Checkup Camp

The SVKM trust decided to organize a screening community health, diagnostic and health care camp on 24 August 2024 at Shirpur predominantly for the tribal in and around Shirpur tehsil. Eminent doctors from Mumbai attended this camp which benefitted more than 1300 people. Diagnostic tests such as blood test, ECG, X-Ray & USG was performed

September’24: Pediatric Diagnostic & Therapeutic Camp

Paediatric surgery camp was organised in collaboration with Shirpur Warvade Municipal Council’s Indira Gandhi Memorial Hospital Shirpur, wherein 48 children were operated for minor surgical procedures. Dermatology camp, a brainchild of Ms. Dweta Patel, was conducted for the school children at Shirpur. The focus was on handling untreated & new patients suffering from various skin ailments Total of 5 schools registered for the camp & more than 120 students availed of this facility.

February’25: Breast & Cervical Cancer Screening

This facility was provided for female healthcare workers (Aanganwadi & Asha Workers) from Shirpur wherein Mammography, Pap Smear & other diagnostic tests were carried out.
